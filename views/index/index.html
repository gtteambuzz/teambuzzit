Mission and Purpose

TEAM Buzz is an organization devoted to making Atlanta better. Through our corporate and campus sponsorships, we are able to support many community-enhancing projects every year.

The concept is simple: provide a service day that acts as a focal point and allows our campus to come together to serve. Together, we work to fulfill our motto of "Progress and Service" with a single plan and strategy to improve our community.
